    “Amidst the nature & the beauty of the world,
    magic seed was planted.”

      ×

    • Spring 2014;
      The Beginning

      The Lemon Tree & Co. Imperial Boat Zamalek

      Once upon a tree, a magic seed was planted in the midst of Egypt’s chaos. It was the beginning of something new; The Lemon Tree & Co. Zamalek Restaurant and Bar rose tall with a magnificent Nile view. Many people thought that The Lemon Tree & Co. was a franchise obtained from abroad; we took pride in starting our very own fully Egyptian brand in the fine hospitality industry.

      ×

    • Summer 2014;
      The Beginning

      The Beach Bar - The Lemon Tree & Co. Telal

      Starting a new Summer season, we introduced our first Summer Venue in the North Coast – The Beach Bar by The Lemon Tree & Co. It was the go-to, classy summer spot located at Telal. It was known best for its soothing Mediterranean ambiance, extensive seasonal menu and uplifting music, all whilst overlooking a striking beach view.

      ×

    • Summer 2014;
      Where Magic Happened

      The Lemon Tree & Co. Marassi

      Making Summer a lot more exciting, we had opened the doors of The Lemon Tree & Co. Marassi, which brought together the best of hospitality and entertainment. It was known for its spectacular events and sumptuous Mediterranean cuisine all summer round. This venue had become the most favored nightlife experience at the North Coast, where guests engaged their every sense without a moment of dullness.

      ×

    • Fall 2014;
      Somewhere Only We Know

      Club Clandestine

      Club Clandestine was a concept created to take our guests on an exclusive surprise dinner or party. Combining mystery and extravagance, planning everything beforehand in secrecy and style; from the invitation and the transpor tation, to delivering a series of extraordinary experiences.

      ×

    • Summer 2015;
      A Connection was found

      Family & Friends

      It all started with our beloved friends and family; uniting together for the love of music. The concept of ‘Family & Friends’ was introduced at The Lemon Tree & Co. Marassi by creating remarkable experiences, where nights turned into mornings and friends turned into family. It has become an ever-growing nightlife brand with talented ar tists from all around the world, offering distinctive music and ever-lasting energy.

      ×

    • Summer 2015;
      Retail

      The Boutique

      Our team of artisans had created an array of products; home wear, décor, apparel and more. In collaboration with local designers, we decided to open a boutique store at the North Coast to offer our guests a specialized shopping experience that would suit their all-time summer tastes.

      ×

    • Spring 2016;
      A Transitional Season

      Beetroot by The Lemon Tree & Co.

      Beetroot by The Lemon Tree & Co. only lasted for one season; it was an oriental-inspired restaurant located by the Nile, offering its guests a rich menu of delicacies. It was a season of transition for The Lemon Tree & Co., a moment where the tree had decided to migrate somewhere else. This is what we call a healthy transition, a new beginning and yet another milestone of our ever-growing tree.

      ×

    • Fall 2016;
      A Place to Call Home

      The Lemon Tree & Co. Katameya

      With passion and ambition, a beautiful seed was planted, creating The Lemon Tree & Co. Katameya, our most iconic restaurant and bar, located in Cairo. Known for its refined experiences and exquisite seasonal-changing menu of delicacies. This place has become a home for us; from its comfortable surroundings and cultivated dining to the company of our dedicated teams and beloved guests.

      ×

    • Spring 2017;
      An Idea Comes to Life

      TLT Events & Entertainment

      Our team started creating events for TLT Concepts, for guests’ social celebrations as well as for corporate renowned entities. TLT Events & Entertainment has grown to become one of the main pillars of our tree, offering the service of event-management and execution and built on the foundation of inspiration and creativity.

      ×

    • Summer 2018;
      A Step into the Wild

      Rituals Beach Club & Restaurant

      Rituals Restaurant & Beach Club was created with the concept of wilderness, playfulness and originality. It is a bohemian-inspired private oasis offering guests a mix of sea, sand and sun, whilst engaging in a series of magnificent activities throughout the day. It has become the ideal spot for a great beach party on the weekends and an ultimate relaxation mood during weekdays.

      ×

    • Summer 2018;
      Crafted with love, appreciated by nature

      Can Limón

      It all started by creating our very own array of décor items and elegant furnishings for our venues. All our pieces and accents were hand-crafted by our talented team of artisans, raising a lot of questions from our guests about each and every piece. Since then, the brand has evolved; becoming ‘Can Limón’, a lifestyle concept store, built on the philosophy of talented craftsman ship, offering a personalized shopping journey, to complement a variety of distinctive tastes. In collaboration with local fashion designers, we have opened our very first concept store, located at Rituals North Coast, followed by Hacienda White and El Gouna. Can Limón is progressively expanding regionally and globally; it will soon open its doors in Cairo, Greece and Ibiza Town.

      ×

    • Summer 2019;
      A Wholehearted Experience

      The Lemon Tree & Co. Hacienda White

      The Lemon Tree & Co. is grounded by nature, fed by art and is hosted by our family at the beautiful Hacienda White in the North Coast. Embracing the nature of simplicity within the beauty of the Mediterranean through an all-white raw interior, a fine selection of subtle greenery, handcrafted accents and direct Sea view. An extensive menu is created to suit the refined tastes of our selected clientele.

      ×

    • 2019;
      An Authentic Story Unfolds

      Le Sidi Boutique Hotel & Restaurant

      Inspired by the idea of honoring the authentic culture in Egypt and the beauty of its generous hospitality. Le Sidi Boutique Hotel & Restaurant is designed to take our guests on a locally inspired journey, giving them a tender feel of identity through the interior design and ambiance on the beautiful ivory sands of the Mediterranean Coast. Our prospect is to serve a finesse clientele with an appetite for authenticity, the ones who appreciate art and refined hospitality.

      ×

    • Summer 2020;
      Rooted in Nature

      Le Sidi Cabanas

      TLT Concepts presents a full-fledged lifestyle experience, Le Sidi Cabanas, an extension to Le Sidi Boutique Hotel & Restaurant. Capturing the true essence of a North African home, the project is meticulously designed and fully furnished, offering a unique living experience with functional luxury and simplicity together under one roof. Le Sidi Cabanas is beautifully set across the golden coast of the Mediterranean at Hacienda Bay North Coast, where comfor t meets luxury and refined hospitality.

      ×

    • 2020;
      A Home outside of Home

      Villa Coconut - El Gouna

      Another natural evolution of TLT Concepts extending into a more intimate brand that feels fresh, soothing and private. Villa Coconut is a home outside of home in every detail, built on the essence of comfort and homelike peacefulness. Inspired by the most versatile fruit, the coconut. This concept is rough on the outside yet soft from the inside, textured yet smooth and mostly adorned in white, reflecting on the identity of El Gouna.

      ×

    • Summer 2021;
      Celebrating a Beautiful Past

      The Origins

      Originating from the very essence of life, that is, culture. The Origins draws its inspiration from the Mediterranean cultures and communities. It’s a new refined hospitality concept, meticulously designed with passion, delicacy and inspiration. It is the perfect spot for families and friends to get away from the bustling city and enjoy contemporary ethnic cuisine in comfortable surroundings.

      ×

    • --;
      The Balearic Lifestyle

      Balearic

      Balearica is our hub for all things music & art. Rising from the Spanish islands, a music app was born to feature music production & radio. It takes a step forward and builds an experience as it showcases special events, while taking you on a tour into the Balearic lifestyle. A real tribute to all the music lovers.

      ×

    • Fall 2021;
      Warm & Wholesome

      TLT Bistro

      Our newest dining concept comes to life at Katameya Heights - TLT Bistro. An experience designed to get together with loved ones with the pairing of a warm and scrumptious meal.

    & a Whole Destiny to Unfold...
