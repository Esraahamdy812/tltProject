A fine hospitality arm of TLT Concepts, presenting a number of refined restaurants, bars, and signature luxury boutique hotels. It is home to extraordinary experiences and culinary expertise, offering simple luxurious life.

Flaunting our beloved brand, The Lemon Tree & Co., by displaying ostentatiously it’s full spectrum. The Lemon Tree & Co. at 1-Ninty will embody our true vision of individualized hospitality and refined entertainment to be; a new standard that can eventually be taken overseas.

Reflecting on the concept of an oasis, The Lemon Tree & Co. at 1-Ninety will represent an enclosed secret garden, portraying elegance yet familiarity. In an attempt to create a tranquil space that brings together a dreamlike garden with great food and drinks, we visualize a bright, architecturally impactful space inspired by the essence of our brand, nature.

Location: One Ninety, New Cairo, Egypt.

The Lemon Tree & Co. is grounded by nature, fed by art and is hosted by our family at the beautiful Hacienda White in the North Coast. It has become our centered home in the North Coast for four consecutive years, a home in which minimalism is embraced through an all-white raw interior, an exquisite selection of subtle greenery, handcrafted accents and a direct Sea view. An extensive menu with an array selection of dishes is harmoniously paired with our selected clientele’s impeccable taste palettes.

The Origins at Garden 8 is a refined hospitality concept by TLT CONCEPTS; an outdoor restaurant and bar with an indoor partition, all of which are meticulously designed with passion, delicacy, and inspiration. It is the perfect spot for families and friends to get away from the bustling city and enjoy contemporary ethnic cuisine in comfortable surroundings. It is an open space of beauty, encouraging families to gather together for a lovely day in the sun. From the morning up until nightfall, The Origins will offer breakfast, lunch, and dinner; an extensive menu of sweet and savory delicacies that will suit the distinctive tastes of our guests.

The concept is derived from one of the most authentic rituals in Egypt; excitedly going out to eat a variety of sumptuous seafood delicacies, with your closest family and friends. Our key objective is to master the traditional Egyptian seafood cuisine with a modern high-end interpretation.

We rejoice Rituals’ unique fusion of Balinese and Mediterranean inspiration. through an awe-inspiring design space, introducing a unique experience and setting, reflecting upon our love for nature.

Rituals Cairo represents a beautiful getaway in a green valley with impressive water features. The interior design is complementing the high ceiling and mezzanines. The design’s foundation is extended from Rituals, Zoya Ghazala Bay, continuously embracing a pattern of raw wood, copper ornaments and beautiful water bodies, complementing the concept of the sea.

Furniture will differ from one place to the other, the main dining area will consist of a number of fine dining tables with comfortable wooden seating for families and friends to gather and feast upon their favourite fish. Whereas, the lounge area’s are designed to be relaxing, laid-back and refreshing; with huge sofas placed between the outdoor and indoor partitions, allowing for daylight and warm sun-rays.

Location: One Ninety, New Cairo, Egypt.

Rituals is a bohemian-inspired private beach club on the North Coast, offering guests a chance to enjoy a perfect mix of sun, sea, and sand, whilst sipping on delicious cocktails and dancing the night away.

Located in the heart of our lifestyle destination, Can Limon, our new concept is a restorative experience of our primal hunting instincts in terms of mimicking our earliest ancestors of the Stone Age in palate, and in a contemporary interpretation of decorum. CAVA; which translates to cave in english, derives its inspiration from the minimal innate nature of cave dwellers who, in the matter of aesthetic, constructed simple structures birthing a more comfortable setting to reside. At CAVA, a simple menu consisting of 6 variations of every item along with crisp white tablecloths and only candles to light the way, stimulating a cosy, calm atmosphere.

Launching Soon

Location: Can Limon, Garden 8, New Cairo.

Emerging from our retail brand, comes Can Limón El Restaurante, an innovative hospitality concept. Architecture is designed so delicately that it fits harmonically with its beautiful interiors of rich wood, walnut beams, and crafted corners. Can Limón El Restaurante will become a 'Living Visual Display' of Can Limón products and aesthetic, offering guests a sensory experience from the moment they step in and until they have had their last sumptuous bites their sweet and savory delights. The Restaurant is so thoughtfully designed that the interiors change with the seasons. With every changing menu, there comes a changing surprise from the quintessence of Can Limón such as our next set of artisan-made serve ware.
