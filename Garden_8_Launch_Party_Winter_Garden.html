Garden 8 Launch Party
2020

Recreating the mesmerizing experience of walking in the main streets of Europe during the Holiday season while taking pictures of every decorated building and enjoying every little light show until you reach the huge tree, Garden 8 metamorphosed to a luxurious winterfest, a euphoria for all the senses pampered with the finest delectables, show-topping merchandise and the jazziest tunes. Revealing for the first time the core foundation and identity of Garden 8, we took our guests on a journey on a different hemisphere, awing them with a full-on production in the garden and outdoor spaces with a Holiday theme.
