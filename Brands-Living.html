The real estate developing arm of TLT Concepts; a natural extension to the simple luxury lifestyle that we continuously ought to deliver. Through combining modern-day functionality and uncompromised luxuries, TLT Living presents an assortment of coastal residences and projects across Egypt and abroad.

Le Sidi Cabanas are fully furnished, hotel-serviced homes, capturing the true essence of a North-African home; it is where comfort meets luxury and refined hospitality. Meticulously designed with utmost care and elegance, offering an authentic and comfortable living experience. Extending from Le Sidi Boutique Hotel & Restaurant, Le Sidi Cabanas are located at Hacienda Bay, Sidi Abdelrahman in the North Coast in Egypt.

Athines By the Sea is the new Athens beach destination, nested amidst the Athenian Riveria, the trendiest metropolitan summer haven in Greece. The Athenian Riviera has become one of the most cosmopolitan and popular destinations in Athens because of its trendy chic vibe and upscale hospitality selection of five-star hotels and restaurants.

Nestled along the spectacular beach of the Red Sea, Can Limon hotel and residences features leveled masses facing the Sea through shifting, layering & clustering.

Articulated volumes of solid & void form a dynamic skyline. Layers of hardscape & landscape connect the rooms to the outdoor spaces. Architecture emerges from the ground in the base of the masses & growing into modern blocks that are treated in simple paint textures. Layers of pattern break the solidity of surfaces and create inter- esting shades that move with the sun.

There are tiny details that brings you a memory from the past but represented in the now. Interiors are filled with tonal colours palette and natural material.

Vegetation has mix between an arid look around some courts and near to buildings but hidden lushes within secret passages reveals a statement of poetic approach of the chosen plant within the context.
